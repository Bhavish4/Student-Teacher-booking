Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How do I schedule an appointment?

Simply log in to your account, search for your teacher, view their availability, and select a convenient time slot. You'll receive an instant confirmation once booked.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel appointments up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any penalty. Simply go to "My Appointments" and select the appropriate option.

How do I receive notifications about my appointments?

You'll receive email and in-app notifications for appointment confirmations, reminders, changes, and cancellations. You can customize your notification preferences in your account settings.

Is the platform free for students?

Yes, the basic version is free for students. Educational institutions typically provide access as part of their services. Premium features may require a subscription.

Can teachers set their own availability?

Absolutely! Teachers have full control over their availability calendar. They can set recurring schedules, block off time, and adjust their availability as needed.

Is my data secure on the platform?

We take data security seriously. All information is encrypted, and we comply with educational privacy regulations. We never share your data with third parties without consent.
